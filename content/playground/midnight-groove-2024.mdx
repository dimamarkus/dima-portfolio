# Midnight Groove 2024

Recorded around the point where the room is locked in and you can push deeper into tension, repetition, and darker textures. This mix leans into hypnotic grooves, restrained transitions, and a more heads-down energy from start to finish.

## Approach

Instead of chasing obvious peaks, the set focuses on momentum and subtle shifts in pressure. Small tonal changes, percussion layers, and longer blends do most of the work.
