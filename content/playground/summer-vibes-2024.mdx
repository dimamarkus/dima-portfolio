# Summer Vibes 2024

This mix leans into warm basslines, bright melodies, and a lighter daytime energy. The goal was to keep the groove steady while letting the set feel open, sunny, and unhurried.

## Highlights

The set starts from a mellow deep-house foundation, then gradually opens into more melodic territory with brighter percussion and more vocal-forward moments.

## Listen

The full recording is on [SoundCloud](https://soundcloud.com/dimamarkus/dima-markus-disorient-country-club-xii-main-stage-2024-05-24-1200am).

## Behind the Scenes

I recorded this set using:

- Pioneer CDJ-3000s
- DJM-900NXS2
- Custom effects chain

The goal was to create a journey that transports listeners to a warm, sunny beach at sunset, while maintaining the sophisticated deep house groove throughout.
